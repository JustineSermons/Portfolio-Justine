Exercise App Design

Role: Designer

Tools: Figma

URL: Figma Link

Date: Spring 2024

Project Description: Exercise App Design allowing users to explore workouts, keep track of their duration and reps when exercising, and see different statistics for their workout progress. A simple design was used for this project for simplicity and ease of use. A light lavender purple color was used to make the workout app have a more relaxing and calming type of feel and look for users who want to workout with peace of mind.

Outcome: Successfully created a simple exercise app design.
